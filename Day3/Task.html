Sai Pallavi

Sai Pallavi Senthamarai Kannan born 9 May 1992 is an Indian actress dancer who primarily works in Telugu, and Malayalam films . South Indian International Movie Awards. She was featured by Forbes magazine as one of India's 30 Under 30 in 2020. After appearing in uncredited roles as a junior artist, she made her major acting debut in the 2015 Malayalam film Premam.

The film ranked as the second highest-grossing Malayalam film at the time and her portrayal of a teacher earned her the Filmfare Award for Best Debut Actress – Malayalam. In 2017, she ventured into Telugu cinema with Fidaa . Pallavi won her first Filmfare Award for Best Actress – Telugu for her performance in the film.
Sai Pallavi was born on 9 May 1992 in a Badaga family in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Her hometown is Kotagiri in the Nilgiris district, Tamil Nadu.Born to Senthamarai Kannan and Radha, she has a younger sister, Pooja Kannan, who has also worked as an actress.She was raised and educated in Coimbatore, doing her schooling at Avila Convent School.
